On the 4th of April 2023, the Equality and Human Rights Commission (EHRC) recommended that the Equality Act use the somewhat vague and meaningless term "biological sex" as a protected characteristic. In response, a petition to not ammend the Equality Act gained sufficient signatures for a debate in Parliament very rapidly, overtaking a contrary petition that had been growing gradually. Given the attitudes of the Government and Opposition, it is arguable that this in itself will achieve very little, and having signed, one should write to one's MP.
