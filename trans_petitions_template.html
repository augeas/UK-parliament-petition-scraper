Following the Supreme Court's ruling on the Equality Act, it is little wonder that some Parliamentary Petitions on trans issues have seen rapid increases in signatures. They all should. The petitions run for 6 months, 100K signatures are required for a debate in Parliament, for whatever utility that may provide. Having signed them, consider writing to your MP, or other representative, as per the advice given by Transactual or TheTransLegalProject. See also the Trans Safety Network and the Trans Advocacy and Complaint Collective. Thanks to WhatTheTrans?! for linking to this page. Members of the trans community are invited to give feedback on this page.
