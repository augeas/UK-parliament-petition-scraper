Following the Supreme Court's ruling on the Equality Act, it is little wonder that Parliamentary Petitions on trans issues have seen rapid increases in signatures. The petitions run for 6 months, 100K signatures are required for a debate in Parliament, for whatever utility that may provide. Having signed them, consider writing to your MP, as per the advice given by TheTransLegalProject. See also The Trans Safety Network and the Trans Advocacy and Complaint Collective.
